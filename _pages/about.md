Hello!

I am Carlos Pantoja-Malaga, an undergraduate student at Florida State University. I am currently studying for a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science and am scheduled to graduate in the spring semester of 2023.

In regard to academia, I am planning on furthering my education through enrolling in graduate school, where I hope to study a subset of Engineering where I could apply my Computer Science background. I am very interested in performing research and utilizing my programming background to assist in a collaborative team setting.

My interest in Computer Science stemmed from my desire to learn how to construct appplications which perform computation analysis. I would like to devote more time into developing such applications, such as computational derivation. Most recently, in a colloborative setting for course COP4521 (Secure, Concurrent, and Parallel Programming in Python), I worked with a partner to develop and deliver a full stack [Python Flask Application](/projects).

My interest in Engineering is founded in my fondness of renewable energy infastructure. I find the implementation and utilization of renewable energy infastructure to be fascinating, and I believe the industry holds imperative significance to the development of present society. 

I do have hobbies outside of crunching numbers and programming. I like to stay active by playing lacrosse as I am very passionate about the sport, and in all aspects of life, I look for opportunities to grow the game. I enjoy folding complex origami, with modular origami being my favorite style.
