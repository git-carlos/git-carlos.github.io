Hello.

I am Carlos Pantoja-Malaga, an undergraduate student at Florida State University. I am currently a candidate for a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, and I am on track to graduate in the spring semester of 2023.

I currently plan to enroll in graduate school, where I hope to study an engineering discipline where I can apply my computer science background. I am interested in research opportunities, as I would like to contribute in a collaborative setting.

Please feel free to review some of my university projects, assignments, and personal work, as I hope you may find some of them interesting. Most recently, for course COP4521 (Secure, Parallel, and Concurrent Programming in Python), a partner and I constructed a [Python Flask Web Application](/projects) served on a Gunicorn instance deployed behind an NGINX proxy server.

Outside of academics, I like to stay active by playing lacrosse, and have been affiliated with the Florida State Club Lacrosse and the Peruvian National Lacrosse Team. I try to grow the game whenever possible and enjoy playing the game with my younger brother the most. I also enjoy folding complex origami, cooking and grilling with my friends, and working on and learning about cars.
